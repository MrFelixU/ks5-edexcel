Scheme of Work - what to teach

  • It's very important that we cover all the Teaching Points outlined in the exam board's Scheme of Work document.
  • When reading the Edexcel scheme, please note that the Specification Reference points at the beginning of each unit are not necessarily to be fully taught in that unit - they are more general. Please go by the OBJECTIVES list - this is the list of objectives that appears in the scheme outline anyway.

Assessments

Students will be sitting a "settling in" assessment in the first week of Year 12. They will then sit an assessment at the end of each half-term. Students who do not perform up the expected level will form the intervention group for the following half-term.
